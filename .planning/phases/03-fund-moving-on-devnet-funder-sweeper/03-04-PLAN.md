Prove the fund->sweep primitive end-to-end on real devnet, exercising the exact-zero and no-double-spend properties as first-class correctness gates.

Output: an opt-in `tests/e2e/` suite behind a `devnet` pytest marker, a session-scoped funded-keypair fixture resilient to airdrop rate limits, and three devnet tests.
</objective>

<objective>
Build the funder — the thinnest possible vault-secret-privileged code path — plus the shared chain-based land-check loop that both funder and sweeper use for no-double-spend confirmation.

Delivers SESS-02 (capped vault->session funding, exact-N semantics D-01), SESS-03 (refuse-before-send when requested cap exceeds MAX_SESSION_CAP, D-03), and the funder half of SEC-02 (funder.py is the single sanctioned importer of vault.py, D-02). Also lands the D-04 insufficient-balance guard and the D-08/D-09 land-check.

Purpose: fund a handed-in session pubkey (D-02 — this module does NOT mint the keystore) while keeping the vault secret's blast radius as small and auditable as possible. When a design choice is ambiguous, prefer the option that shrinks the vault-secret surface (the phase's guiding steer).

Output: `bastion/funder.py`, `bastion/land_check.py`, `bastion/fund_errors.py`, a new `RpcClient.get_signature_statuses` method, unit tests, and the vault-isolation allowlist extension.
</objective>
